Mapping Marine Diversity

Explore the hidden patterns of life beneath the waves. This visualization uses data from the Ocean Biodiversity Information System (OBIS) to model ecological communities across different ocean depths.

How to use:

  • Explore the global distribution of marine life.
  • Click anywhere on the map to select a location.
  • Scroll down to "dive" into the water column and see how diversity changes with depth.
